The Nile Delta

Egyptian cotton only grows in a small area called the Nile Delta. The Nile Delta's proximity to the mediterranean coupled with its fertile lands make the weather and soil ideal for the cultivation of the world's finest cotton.

Integrity

Planted in the cool month of March to protect the young plants from the hot Egyptian Sun, and harvested by hand over 5 stages to ensure ideal maturity in September. The harvest ensures no defoliants or other chemicals usually used in mechanical harvesting ever touch the cotton, for the extra long fibres of Egyptian cotton to remain intact and undamaged, ready to be spun into the finest yarns and fabrics.

The Fibre

Egyptian cotton has the world's longest, most absorbent and strongest fibres creating the softest and most durable fabric.
